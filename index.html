Crystals Collector



There are four crystals below. Each crystal represents a different number of points that is hidden from you until you click on it. By clicking on each crystal, you add those points to your score.

You win the game by matching your score to the random number. You lose if your score goes over the random number.

Each time the game starts over, the crystals' values and the random number change.

In short, this is kind of a weird version of Blackjack!

Press any key to start playing.
